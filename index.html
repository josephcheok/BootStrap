About Me

I am a professional accountant by trade but an analytical problem solver at heart. That was the part of my work I really enjoyed. I had an opportunity while I was an accountant at a power grid construction company to standardise the reporting process for project engineers on Excel. I programmed some of the repetitive steps project enginners performed to prepare their monthly reports into push type button macros using Visual Basic, saving their time. Having very little background in programming, I bridged the knowledge gap to enable what I do through YouTube and online forums. Once I have created an automated reporting template for the engineers and my assignment was over, I was hungry for more!

I also run a fully customisable apparel shop online called . What we do is we allow the customer to draw up their own t-shirt, bag, tote or leggings using our templates. We then get the customer to take a shot of the artwork on our template, I would then refine and digitise the artwork using Photoshop and Illustrator before transferring the art over to fabric, which is then cut up and sewn to the customer's size and taste. I also had an opportunity to dwelve into website construction with the help of the tools provided by Shopify. But there is a lot more things that I want to do on the website that go beyond their basic tools!
